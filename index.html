Our Mission

E-Responde is a smart emergency alert and crime reporting system designed to enhance community safety through real-time communication between civilians and law enforcement. The system combines mobile applications, smartwatch integration, and AI-powered analytics to provide a comprehensive safety solution.

Key Features

Our platform enables users to report crimes with geotagging, send instant SOS alerts through multiple activation methods, and communicate directly with assigned police officers via VoIP calling. The system features AI-powered threat detection and ARIMA-based crime trend forecasting for proactive resource allocation.
